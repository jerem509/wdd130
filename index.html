Haiti

Haiti, the Republic of Haiti, is a Caribbean island nation on Hispaniola, with Port-au-Prince as its capital and largest city, known for its rich culture, history, and challenges, including political instability and economic hardship. It is located on the island of Haiti and is a two-thirds mountainous country. The official languages are French and Haitian Creole
